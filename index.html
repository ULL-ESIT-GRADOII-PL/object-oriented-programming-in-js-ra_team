Measure Converter


• Usage of this Measure Converter:

To use the measure converter you just need to write the value, the source measure and the output measure and then press 'Enter' (carriage return) and the converted measure'll be displayed.


• Types of conversions with examples:

1. From Celsius to Farenheit or Kelvin (32C to F, 42C to K; 32C F, 42C K)
2. From Farenheit to Celsius or Kelvin (32F to C, 42F to K; 32F C, 32F K)
3. From Kelvin to Celsius or Farenheit (32K to C, 42K to F; 32K C 42K F)
4. From Meters to Inches (32M to I; 32M I)
5. From Inches to Meters (32I to M; 32I M)
